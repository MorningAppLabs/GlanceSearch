# GlanceSearch

**See it. Select it. Do anything with it.**

GlanceSearch is a blazing fast, system-wide floating search utility for Windows. Instantly capture any region of your screen, extract text automatically, and take action—all without leaving your current context.

## 🚀 Features

- **Instant Capture:** Hit `Ctrl+Shift+G` anywhere to pause the screen and select a region.
- **Smart OCR:** Instantly extracts and copies text from your selection.
- **Smart Actions:** Auto-detects URLs, Emails, Phone Numbers, and QR/Barcodes.
- **Inline Translation:** Translate anything on your screen directly in the floating panel.
- **Visual Search:** Easily search for images and text across Google, Bing, or DuckDuckGo.
- **Privacy-First:** All text extraction happens completely offline on your PC by default.
- **History:** Keep track of your past captures, text, and translations.

## 💾 Installation

1. Head over to the [Releases](https://github.com/MorningAppLabs/GlanceSearch/releases) page.
2. Download `GlanceSearchSetup.exe`.
3. Run the installer. 
4. GlanceSearch will open silently in your system tray. Press `Ctrl+Shift+G` to start!

## ⚙️ How to Use

1. Press `Ctrl+Shift+G` (or your custom hotkey) to dim the screen.
2. Click and drag to select a rectangle, or hold shift for freehand selection.
3. Once captured, a floating action panel appears.
4. From here, you can:
   - Copy the extracted text
   - Search the web
   - Translate the text inline
   - Save to history
   - Open detected links or emails

## 🛡️ Privacy

We believe your screen is your business. 
- **OCR is local:** By default, GlanceSearch uses the built-in Windows OCR engine. No data leaves your computer to extract text.
- **Explicit Actions:** Data is only sent to external services (like Google or Translation APIs) when you explicitly click a button to perform that action.
